Biography

I had exchange experiences at The Chinese University of Hong Kong and Renmin University of China. Currently, I am actively seeking employment opportunities.

Publications

"Investigating Time Management in the Age of Mobile Phones: A Perspective of Activity Theory and Affordance", Master Thesis. PDF
Using an auto-ethnographic research method, the study analyzes how users manage time with the intervention of mobile phones
"Using Media Dependency Theory and Theory of Rational Action: Predicting Public Health Behavior", National Chengchi University PDF
Through the use of database data, the research employs data analysis methods to study the impact of social media on users' health information reception behaviors
"Consumerism Strategies in Anti-Feminist Discourse on Social Media", Taiwan Information Annual Action Network Seminar, November 2021, Paper Presenter PDF
Using discourse analysis, the study analyzes the discursive strategies and implications of feminist and anti-feminist rhetoric on the internet.
"Framing Analysis of 'People's Daily' Reporting on the COVID-19 Outbreak from the Perspective of Legitimacy", Chinese Communication Association Annual Seminar, June 2021, Paper Presenter PDF
Using framing analysis, the research studies media propaganda during specific events, revealing the underlying ideologies of the text.
"Communicator or Intermediary? A Preliminary Exploration of the Social Relationships and Meaning Production between Users and the Virtual Assistant Siri from the Perspective of Human-Machine Communication", Taiwan Information Annual Artificial Intelligence Seminar, November 2020, Paper Presenter PDF
Using in-depth interviews, the research analyzes AI product users' preferences, motivations, and experiences
